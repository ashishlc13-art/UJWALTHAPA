Project Report

Lead: Aashish Lamichhane
Partners: D Go x Ujwal Thapa Foundation
Date: April 2026

1. Executive Summary

Following the strategic partnership with the Ujwal Thapa Foundation, our team led the end-to-end branding, marketing, and distribution of the 1-hour and 50-minute documentary Dimag Ghochne Manche. This project served as the Soft Launch for D Go’s new Logo and Name, positioning the platform as a socially responsible leader in the Nepali OTT space.

Through professional coordination, we achieved massive visibility across 16 media outlets with Zero Marketing Spend, effectively saving the company significant capital while building high brand equity.

2. Strategic Branding & Creative Execution

The campaign was built on three professional pillars:

  • Visual Identity: Partnered with the BLQ team to produce high-quality social media assets that introduced the new D Go branding.
  • Engagement: Produced and distributed 5 targeted video clips highlighting the documentary’s core message to drive organic sharing.
  • Editorial PR: Drafted and distributed a nationwide press release focusing on "Reality over Pranks," securing organic news slots instead of paid advertisements.
