The Systemic Mutation

Ujwal believed Nepal didn't need a "Hero" or a "Strongman."

It needed a Systemic Mutation of the collective DNA.

He called this "The Positive Virus."

"I hope in the coming year, you will die. Die that life you led so far, following someone else's path and be re-born with this passion to lead your life your own way."
